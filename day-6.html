Day 6 — Social Warm-Up Arsenal

Nervous system still screaming on cold approaches? We're about to desensitize it. Mission: three tiers of low-stakes operations that prove most strangers are neutral or friendly, not predators.

The Warm-Up Ladder

Tier 1: acknowledge existence. Tier 2: 30-60 second micro-chat. Tier 3: 3-5 minute genuine conversation. Today we execute Tier 1 five times.

1 / Tier 1 – "Acknowledge & Move" Challenge

Target: five brief acknowledgments before midnight. No compliments, no follow-ups—just eye contact, smile, "morning," advance.
